The Problem & Our Solution

The Problem

The universe is vast, and the data collected from telescopes is even vaster. Sifting through terabytes of light curve data to find a single, faint signature of a transiting exoplanet is a monumental task that can take years of manual analysis. Humanity's quest to find a second home is bottlenecked by the sheer volume of information.

Our Solution

We are **StellarX**. We built a machine learning platform that automates the analysis of astronomical data. Our models can process a year's worth of telescopic data in minutes, identifying planetary candidates with unprecedented accuracy and speed. We don't just find planets; we accelerate the future of space exploration.
